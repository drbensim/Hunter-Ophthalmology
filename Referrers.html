Information For Referrers

Hunter Ophthalmology sincerely thanks you for entrusting us with your patients. In return, we are committed to providing them with the highest standard of care, delivered with respect, compassion, and genuine attention to their individual needs.

How to refer your patient

You can refer your patients to us through several means:

  • Oculo
  • By sending your referral to our email address (reception@hunterophthalmology.com.au)

Urgent consultations

If you require an urgent consultation, please give our rooms a call or text the doctor if you have his mobile number and please send your referral in to us via the above means and we will see your patient as soon as possible.

Contact and feedback

Feel free to give us a call on (02) 4106 9999 if you have any issues and/ or the doctor if you have their mobile contact. We gladly welcome any feedback on our service!
